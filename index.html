About Me

I'm Shiv, an engineer in the purest sense. Not only of software but of systems, habits, ideas, and self.

I see life as architecture. Everything I do, from learning and lifting to thinking and building, gets broken down, optimized, and rebuilt. To me, nothing is random. Everything is design.

I chase clarity. I chase structure. I chase mastery.

I believe good engineering begins with asking good questions—not just how things work, but why they should exist at all. I care about the invisible things: the principles beneath the product, the mindset behind the method.

I'm driven by the idea that growth, real growth, is simply systems thinking applied to life. Code teaches that. Philosophy teaches that. Failure teaches that, too.

Software engineering just happens to be my medium.

But at the core, I'm someone trying to build clean systems in my work, in my habits, and within myself.
